FILM POSTERS

The Perks Of Being a Wallflower
Juno

Film has always been a huge source of inspiration for me,
so I decided to create two posters to pay tribute to some of
my favourite films.

Screen One

Inspired by the grid-layout of Josef Muller-Brockman, this
is my design for a poster for one of my all time favourite films
(and books), Stephen Chbosky's "The Perks of Being a Wallflower".

I created a geometric shape to be the main focus of the
poster, which resembles a flower and is made up from many
overlapped infinity signs, a reference to one of the most
important lines of the book- "And in that moment, I
swear we were infinite.".

I feel that the sense of movement given by this infinity
sign reflects the anxious feeling that Charlie seems to have
in the film- he is being swept up in the events of the lives
of those around him.

Screen Two

I wanted the design of this poster to reflect the strikingly
realistic and bizarre comedy that is a feature of the film.
Even the visual imagery is a reference to the line from the film -

"How did you even generate enough pee for three pregnancy tests? That's amazing...".
